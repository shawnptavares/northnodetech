About North Node Tech

North Node Tech is a reflection of over a decade of hands-on experience in building, securing, and scaling IT infrastructure. Founded by Shawn Tavares, a seasoned systems engineer, the brand represents a commitment to reliable cloud consulting, automation through scripting, and grounded infrastructure design.

From managing datacenter systems and SAN environments to deploying Microsoft Intune and Azure solutions, North Node Tech bridges the gap between legacy systems and future-ready architecture. Whether you're looking to modernize, automate, or secure your environment, North Node Tech brings technical clarity with a calm, forest-rooted mindset.

Inspired by Nature

Just like a forest ecosystem, I believe IT systems should be balanced, resilient, and quietly powerful. That’s why North Node Tech takes inspiration from nature’s architecture — layered, adaptive, and built to endure.
